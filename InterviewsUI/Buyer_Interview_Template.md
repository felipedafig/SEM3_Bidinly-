### Scenario 1: Account Creation and First Impression

**Task:** "You want to use Bidinly to buy a property. Start by creating an account."

**Steps:**
1. Open the homepage
2. Navigate to sign up
3. Create a Buyer account
4. Log in

**Follow-up Questions:**
- What was your first impression of the homepage?
- Was anything confusing during sign-up?
- Did you understand what you could do after logging in?
- Was the difference between Buyer and Agent roles clear?

---

### Scenario 2: Browsing Properties and Placing a Bid

**Task:** "Find a property you like and place a bid on it."

**Steps:**
1. Browse or search for properties
2. Apply basic filters (city, bedrooms, bathrooms, size)
3. Open a property detail page
4. Review property information
5. Place a bid (with amount, deal, and expiry date)
6. Check that the bid appears in your bids list

**Follow-up Questions:**
- How easy was it to find a suitable property?
- Was placing a bid clear and intuitive?
- What information was missing, if any?
- Did you understand what the "Deal" field was for?
- How clear was the bid expiry date concept?
- Was the property detail page helpful?
